If You Know Basic PySpark

These analytics patterns are common in every data team: "how many of our January cohort were still active in April?" (retention), "which channel drives the highest LTV?" (attribution). You can do them with DataFrame groupBy + pivot, but Spark SQL window functions are more expressive, more readable to stakeholders, and often faster because Catalyst can optimize them better.

Reading Cohort Tables

A cohort table reads row-by-row: "Of the 50 customers who signed up in January (row 1), 48 ordered in month 0 (same month as signup), 35 ordered in month 1, 20 in month 2..." The diagonal shows the same calendar month across different cohorts — useful for separating seasonal effects from retention improvements.
